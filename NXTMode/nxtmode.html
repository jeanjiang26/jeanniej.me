NXTMODE

NXTMODE is an innovative fashion-tech company dedicated to transforming the fashion industry through cutting-edge e-commerce solutions. By connecting emerging designers with fashion enthusiasts, the company aims to foster creativity, innovation, and accessibility in the market.

NXTMODE emphasizes user-centric design and exceptional user experiences, leveraging technologies such as Shopify, ReactJS, NodeJS, GraphQL, and more to build a powerful platform. As a recipient of the IYA Ignite Grant, NXTMODE has positioned itself as a disruptor in the fashion marketplace, revolutionizing how designers and consumers interact.

During my time at NXTMode, I worked closely with my team members and our stakeholder to develop the iOS application, enhancing my expertise in iOS development and full-stack technologies while delivering impactful solutions for the fashion industry.
